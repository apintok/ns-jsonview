# Privacy Policy — NetSuite JSON View

**Last updated:** June 2026

## Overview

NetSuite JSON View ("the Extension") is a Chrome extension that formats and displays JSON stored in NetSuite Text Area fields. This policy explains how the Extension handles information.

## Data collection

**The Extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or usage data.**

All processing happens locally in your browser on pages you visit. No information is sent to the developer or to any third-party service.

## What the Extension accesses

The Extension runs only on NetSuite websites (`*.netsuite.com` and `*.app.netsuite.com`). On those pages it:

- Reads the content of Text Area fields that contain valid JSON
- Displays that content in a formatted tree view
- Copies formatted JSON to your clipboard when you click **Copy** (clipboard access is local to your device)

The Extension does not access other websites, browser history, bookmarks, cookies, or NetSuite data outside of the field content needed to provide this feature.

## Permissions

| Permission | Purpose |
|------------|---------|
| `host_permissions` for `*.netsuite.com` | Run the content script only on NetSuite pages |

The Extension does not request `storage`, `tabs`, or other broad permissions.

## Third parties

The Extension does not use analytics, advertising, authentication, or external APIs. No data is sold, rented, or shared with third parties.

## Data retention

Because no data is collected, there is no data retention or deletion process beyond what NetSuite and your browser already manage for page content.

## Children

The Extension is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from anyone.

## Changes

This policy may be updated if the Extension changes. The "Last updated" date at the top will be revised when that happens.
